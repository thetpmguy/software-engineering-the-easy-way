# 🧭 Learning Paths

The full course, in order, is the best experience. But if you have a specific goal or
limited time, these curated shortcuts are safe — they include every module the later
ones depend on.

> Whatever path you take, read [START HERE](START_HERE.md) first (5 minutes).

---
